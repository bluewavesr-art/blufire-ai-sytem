# TikTok Agent — Global Roofing Company

## Account
Global Roofing Company TikTok (managed by Blufire)

## Content Strategy
Low frequency (1-2 per week when compelling content exists). Focus on:
- Hail damage identification videos
- "This is what a bad roofing job looks like" exposé content

Content (15-45 seconds): Quick, educational, visually interesting
CTA: "Follow for more DFW roofing tips" or "Get a free inspection"

## Don't Post If
There is no compelling visual content. TikTok without good video is a waste.
