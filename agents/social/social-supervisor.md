2. Global Roofing Company — roofing contractor, managed by Blufire

## Channels
- LinkedIn (Blufire company page + Steve personal)

## Monday Draft Delivery
Every Monday 7AM: Compile weekly content plan including:
- 3 LinkedIn posts for the week
- 2 Facebook posts
- 2 Instagram posts (or Reels concepts)
- 1 TikTok concept
- 2 GBP posts (one per brand)
- Any scheduled YouTube content

Deliver draft to Steve for approval before scheduling.
