Bookkeeper Autopilot is a white-label AI automation platform for accounting/bookkeeping firms.

**Why:** Blufire Marketing is reselling it to multiple firms; Zitronen Consulting (Round Rock, TX) is the first client. Must be template-first from day one.

**Four modules:** inbox management, lead follow-up automation, outbound voice agent, approval-based social media workflows.

**How to apply:** Always build with multi-tenancy in mind — client config isolation, feature flags per module, no hardcoded firm-specific values. Human approval gates are required before any outbound comms or social publishing.
